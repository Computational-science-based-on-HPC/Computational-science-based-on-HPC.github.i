libphysics

Description

libphysics is a physics library that simulates various physics calculations as parallelized and serialized implementations to obtain optimal performance and results.

Simulations

1. Damped spring motion

  • Damped Spring Motion is a simple harmonic motion that simulates the motion of a mass attached to a spring and oscillates in a y-axis direction with given parameters that the user specifies like mass and stiffness of the spring.
  • This simulation is implemented in both parallel and serial implementations.

2. Elastic Pendulum

  • Elastic pendulum/pendulum spring/swinging spring system is a system where it simulates the motion of 2D-spring in both directions y-axis and x-axis = given parameters that the user specifies like mass and stiffness of the spring.
  • Due to the chaotic motion of this system and its dependencies, its algorithm is implemented in the serial algorithm only.

3. 1D Heat Equation

  • Heat Equation is an equation where it simulates the change of heat in a body isolated from the outer world and isn't affected by any other heat or cooling source.
  • In this simulation, we simulate heat propagation in a 1D body as a wire and the change in its temperature over time using Fourier transform.
  • This simulation is implemented as a serial and parallel program.

4. 2D Heat Equation

  • Heat Equation is an equation where it simulates the change of heat in a body isolated from the outer world and isn't affected by any other heat or cooling source.
  • In this simulation, we simulate heat propagation in a 2D body as a CPU as a square or rectangle and the change in its temperature over time using Fourier transform.
  • This simulation is implemented as a serial and parallel program.
